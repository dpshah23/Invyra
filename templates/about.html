Project Overview

Invyra is a cutting-edge B2B Micro-SaaS platform designed to automatically analyze vendor invoices using advanced AI technology and detect fraud before payment approval. The platform provides finance teams with comprehensive risk assessment and blockchain-verified invoice authenticity.

Key Features

🤖 AI-Powered Analysis

Automatic extraction of invoice data and intelligent pattern recognition to identify fraudulent activities.

🔍 Real-Time Fraud Detection

Detect duplicates, modified bank accounts, inflated amounts, fake vendors, and tampered PDFs instantly.

⛓️ Blockchain Verification

Tamper-proof blockchain proof of invoice authenticity with immutable verification records.

📊 Risk Scoring System

Advanced risk scoring (0-100) with detailed explanations before payment approval.

📈 Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive statistics, charts, and trends to track invoice processing and fraud detection rates.

🔎 Advanced Filtering

Search and filter invoices by status, risk level, vendor, and more.

🚨 Fraud Detection Capabilities

Duplicate Invoices

Identifies invoices that have been submitted multiple times to prevent double payments.

Modified Bank Details

Detects when bank account numbers differ from historical vendor payment information.

Inflated Amounts

Flags invoices with amounts significantly higher than vendor averages or previous quotes.

Fake Vendors

Identifies vendors not found in the approved vendor database.

Tampered PDFs

Analyzes PDF metadata to detect recent modifications to financial details.
